    23rd General Convocation 2023 at Rajarata University

    As the Media Team of Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, we had the privilege to document and commemorate the Convocation Ceremony of 2024, an event that marked the culmination of years of dedication and hard work for the graduates.

    Our team diligently captured the essence of this significant milestone, showcasing the joy, pride, and excitement that filled the air as graduates crossed the stage to receive their well-earned degrees. Through our lens, we immortalized the emotions, the camaraderie, and the sense of accomplishment that resonated throughout the ceremony.
